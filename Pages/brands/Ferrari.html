Ferrari 250 GTO

The 1963 Ferrari 250 GTO is a legendary and highly valuable V12-powered sports car, known for its racing success and scarcity, with only 36 ever built between 1962 and 1964. Its design, a collaboration between Giotto Bizzarrini and Sergio Scaglietti, is considered timeless, contributing to its status as one of the most desirable collector cars, with some private sales reaching over $70 million.

Ferrari 335 Sport Scaglietti

The 1957 Ferrari 335 Sport Scaglietti is a legendary and rare sports racing car known for its powerful V12 engine, high top speed (over 180 mph), and historical racing success. One of only four built, it was designed to compete with Maserati and features a sleek, aerodynamic body by Scaglietti. This model is highly sought after, with one car selling for over $35 million at a 2016 auction, making it one of the most expensive cars ever sold at public auction at that time.

LaFerrari Aperta

The 2017 LaFerrari Aperta is a rare, open-top hypercar that combines a V12 engine with a hybrid system for a total output of 963 horsepower. Only 210 units were produced to celebrate Ferrari's 70th anniversary. The Aperta maintains the coupe's performance, acceleration (0-100 km/h in under 3 seconds), and torsional rigidity, while offering the thrill of open-air driving. It features a removable carbon-fiber roof and various design tweaks for the convertible experience.
