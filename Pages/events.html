About the Event

Join us for the annual Vintage Car Exhibition, a celebration of automotive history and mechanical art. Since its founding in 1985, Wheels on Fire has gathered enthusiasts from across the globe to witness some of the most iconic and rare vehicles ever built.

This year, the exhibition brings together over 120 legendary cars, including masterpieces such as the Ferrari 250 GTO, Ford GT40, and early electric prototypes. Visitors will have the opportunity to explore restored classics, limited-edition models, and vehicles that shaped entire eras of racing and engineering.

Alongside the main exhibition, guests can enjoy interactive showcases, live engine demonstrations, and special guest appearances from renowned collectors and restorers. Whether you're a seasoned expert or a newcomer to car culture, this event promises an unforgettable experience.
